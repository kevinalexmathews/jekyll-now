Kevin Alex Mathews

I am pursuing Ph.D. at the Natural Language Processing group in Heidelberg Institute for Theoretical Studies (HITS), Heidelberg, Germany under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Michael Strube.

Prior to that, I was pursuing M.S. (by Research) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM), Chennai, India under the guidance of Prof. Dr. P Sreenivasa Kumar.

At IITM, I worked on knowledge representation, ontology learning and text mining. Currently, I am interested in building datasets for metonymy (a figure of speech) and models for resolving metonymy in text.
